**Dynamic Heterogeneous Graph Representation Learning Survey** *(Oct. 2025 – Jan. 2026)*
- Established a unified formalism and algorithm-centric taxonomy for Dynamic Heterogeneous Graph representation learning, characterizing temporal modeling biases across embedding-, GNN-, and Transformer-based methods.

**Masked Autoencoder for Dynamic Heterogeneous Graphs** *(Apr. 2025 – Aug. 2025)*
- Proposed a self-supervised framework using a Masked Autoencoder to model spatiotemporal dynamics without manual labels. A hierarchical attention mechanism integrates heterogeneous structure and temporal evolution through reconstruction tasks, achieving 1–4% AUC gains on real-world link prediction.

- Designed a heterogeneous temporal hypergraph network (HTHGN) to capture group interactions beyond pairwise relations via P-uniform hyperedges and hierarchical attention, improving representation of dynamic structures.
