I am a PhD Candidate in the School of Cyberspace at [Hangzhou Dianzi University](https://www.hdu.edu.cn), advised by [Prof. Pengfei Jiao](https://cspjiao.github.io/). I received my M.S. in Computer Science and Technology from Hangzhou Dianzi University (2024) and my B.S. in Computer Science and Technology from Shihezi University (2021).

My research focuses on **Graph Learning**, with particular interest in **Dynamic Heterogeneous Graphs** and **Foundation Models** for graph-structured temporal data.
